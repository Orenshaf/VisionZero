In 2014 the Mayor of New York City, Bill de Blasio, created a program called the 'Vision Zero Initiative'. He based the initiaitive on a a similar one done in Sweden. The overall idea behind Vision Zero is that deaths and injuries are not actually accidents and instead they are a result of poor civic planning.
